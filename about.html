The Man of the Hour

Five decades of stories, achievements, and cherished memories

Michael James Anderson

Entrepreneur • Mentor • Friend • Family Man

Born in Boston on March 15, 1976, Michael has always been a force of nature. From his early days building tree forts in his parents' backyard to founding his first tech startup at 28, he's never been one to shy away from a challenge.

After graduating from MIT with a degree in Computer Science, Michael spent his twenties working at various startups in Silicon Valley before returning to Boston to launch his own venture. Anderson Technologies, founded in 2004, grew from a three-person operation in his garage to a company of over 200 employees.

But Michael's greatest achievements aren't found on a balance sheet. He's a devoted husband to Sarah for 22 years, father to three incredible children—Emma (19), James (16), and Olivia (12)—and a mentor to countless young professionals in the Boston tech scene.

When he's not leading his company or coaching his daughter's soccer team, you'll find Michael on the golf course, experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen, or planning the family's next adventure. He's visited 47 countries and has stories from each one.
